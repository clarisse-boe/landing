Secrets of french cheeses

See in video

Today, the cheese-making tradition in France is strongly rooted, with more than 90,000 dairy farms and 3,000 cheese producers, all with their own know-how. With an average of 40 heads for 60 hectares of land, French dairy farms are of a human scale. Cheese is therefore part of the French culture and there are restaurants exclusively dedicated to cheese.
